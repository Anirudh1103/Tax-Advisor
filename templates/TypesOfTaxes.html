Types of Taxes in India

Type of Tax Description
Income Tax Tax levied on the income of individuals and corporations.
Corporate Tax Tax levied on the profits of companies.
Capital Gains Tax Tax levied on the profit or loss from the sale of assets like property or stocks.
Wealth Tax Tax levied on the net wealth of individuals.
Gift Tax Tax levied on the value of gifts received.
Professional Tax Tax levied on the income of professionals like doctors, lawyers, and chartered accountants.
Goods and Services Tax (GST) A multi-level tax levied on the supply of goods and services.
Customs Duty Tax levied on imported goods.
Excise Duty Tax levied on the manufacture of goods within the country.
Service Tax Tax levied on the provision of services.
Stamp Duty Tax levied on legal documents.
Value Added Tax (VAT) Tax levied on the value addition at each stage of production and distribution.
